Usage

This app is crafted to assist students in learning mathematics in a personalized way. It uses advanced AI to adapt to each student's learning preferences and habits.

Getting started is straightforward: simply sign in, tell the chatbot about your study preferences and the mathematical topics you're interested in, and begin your learning session.

Remember to create an account and log in so the app can track your progress, save your learning preferences, and tailor future lessons for the most effective learning experience.
